Watch My Workout

Mobile, Smartwatch

WatchMyWorkout is an Android phone application with an Android Wear smartwatch companion app. The smartwatch app is used to track a user's activity, such as running or weight lifting. The data is saved on the smartwatch until it is connected to the phone app. The main focus of the phone app is to show the user his or her progress over a period of time, thus motivating them to continue exercising. The phone app also allows a user to set an exercise schedule. By completing this project I gained more knowledge about Android development and Android Wear interfaces and the sensors available to the watch.

Appli

Web, Mobile

Appli is a mobile and web app that I developed with my classmate Paul Conroy last year for our third year project. It is a job seeking application where an employer can post a job listing using the web application and a job seeker can apply to a listing with the mobile app. The goal of appli was to connect a job seeker to an employer and create a bridge for new opportunities. We implemented the “Tinder” mechanic on the mobile app, where a user is displayed a job listing with all the necessary information they need to know if they’re interested in the job. The user would then swipe the listing right or left to save it to their profile or dismiss it respectively. The next listing would then be displayed to the user and the process repeats.

App of Holding

Android

This application allows a player of a tabletop RPG to easily manage their characters inventory without the use of pen and paper. The app allows a player to add an item to their inventory, store gold values, automatically calculate encumbrance and sell items. I begun development in early April 2017 and soon released the first version to the Google play store. So far it has received great feedback from the community, and at the time of writing has over 12,500 downloads. I plan to continue development and add many of the features suggested by fans.
